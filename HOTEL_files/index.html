About Us

Monika Hotel & Success Family Restaurant is located in the beautiful area of Bansgadhi, Kanchan 1, Lausa, Nepal. We offer a warm, welcoming environment perfect for families, business travelers, and tourists. Our restaurant serves delicious, home-cooked meals that everyone will enjoy, while our hotel rooms are designed for your comfort and relaxation. We are a family-friendly hotel that provides outstanding customer service.

At Monica Hotel, we believe in supporting local businesses, offering job opportunities to the community, and ensuring the satisfaction of every guest that walks through our doors. We also understand the importance of preserving our cultural heritage, and we strive to share the flavors and hospitality of Nepal with visitors from around the world.

Our Services

  • Comfortable Rooms: We offer well-furnished rooms equipped with modern amenities, including Wi-Fi, air-conditioning, and more.
  • Delicious Food: Our restaurant provides a variety of Nepalese and international dishes, with fresh ingredients and authentic flavors.
  • Event Hosting: We have space to host events, meetings, and small gatherings with all the necessary facilities and catering options.
  • Friendly Staff: Our team of experienced and friendly staff is always ready to make your stay as comfortable and pleasant as possible.

Our Location

Monika Hotel & Success Family Restaurant is located in Bansgadhi, Kanchan 1, Lausa, Nepal, a serene and beautiful area surrounded by nature. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, our hotel offers easy access to local attractions and transportation hubs.

For more details about our services, location, and other offerings, feel free to visit our official website.

How to Get Here

  • By Car: We are located a short 20-minute drive from the main highway, easily accessible by taxi or private car.
  • By Bus: The nearest bus station is just a 10-minute walk from the hotel, with frequent buses to and from major cities.
